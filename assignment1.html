Laser Cut Press Fit Construction Kit!

How I completed this assignment: I first began this assignment by familiarising myself with what a press kit is. I then used the calipers to measure the thickness of my carboard. I wrote it down and began thinking of a kit design. Once I had my design I added in the spaces I wanted to connect using the thickness of the cardboard to ensure the pieces would hold together. Since I this is my first time laser cutting I reached out to Hannah, she has experience laser cutting.
