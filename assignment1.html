Laser Cut Press Fit Construction Kit!


How I completed this assignment:
I first began this assignment by familiarising myself with what a press kit is. I then used the calipers to measure the thickness of my carboard. I wrote it down and began thinking of a kit design. Once I had my design I added in the spaces I wanted to connect using the thickness of the cardboard to ensure the pieces would hold together. Since I this is my first time laser cutting,I reached out to Hannah since she has experience laser cutting. She explained to me how to use the lasercutter. I created two test pieces to check my dimensions and ensure the pieces would hold. I was fortunate enough that my pieces work and I moved forward with printing the remaining pieces.
