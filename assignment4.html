Moving Parts!

How I completed this assignment:

I first began this assignment by thinking of some ideas of moving parts. I had an idea of using two of the screw Joshua gave us in class to hold two gears together on some sort of stabilizer. I figured you move one gear and it will move the other. I designed my pieces in OnShape. I created a circle and followed the curvature of the circle I made and added in the shape I wanted for the gear teeth. I left enough space between the teeth so the teeth on the other gear would fit into the empty space. When I was done with the basic shape of my gear I extruded it to .25 inches. I then created the stabilizing piece. I create a rectangle with curved edges and added in a hole for the screw to fit into using the same size as the one used in the gear. Once I was happy with what I came up with I exported my work to a dxf file to open with Illustrator. Once in Illustrator I followed the steps to lasercut my pieces out.

Challenges:

I actually had no issues with this assignment. My piece came out perfectly on the first try.

Laser Cutter Settings

  • Power: 100%
